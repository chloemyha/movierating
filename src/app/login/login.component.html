Rotten Strawberries
Rotten Strawberries is an American review-aggregation website for film and television. The company was launched in August 1998 by three undergraduate students at the University of California, Berkeley: Senh Duong, Patrick Y. Lee, and Stephen Wang. Rotten Less than 60% of reviews for a movie or TV show are positive. Certified fresh A special distinction awarded to the best reviewed movies and TV Shows. Fresh At least 60% of reviews for a movie or TV show are positive. Rotten Less than 60% of reviews for a movie or TV show are positive. On the review aggregator website Rotten Tomatoes, a film has a rating of 100% if each professional review recorded by the website is assessed as positive rather than negative.
